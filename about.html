ABOUT US

At EDGE, we believe that every moment, big or small, is infinitely precious and deserves to be captured. Our team of professional photographers and videographers are handpicked and trained by the world’s most sought-after artists and photographers, our unique style coupled with our passion for high quality commercial and family photography has helped us gain many high-profile clients in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

Our professional photography studio provides services across a range of disciplines, from portrait photography, food and drink, weddings and engagement, real estate, corporate events and so much more. Our accomplished photographers are equipped with the technical training and artistic vision that allows them to capture the intricate beauty and personality of their subject and clients.
With over a decade of photography experience in the UAE, we possess invaluable local knowledge and have established ourselves as a leading brand that specializes in event photography, creative fashion photography, wedding photography, artistic family photography, and baby photography. It doesn’t end there – our dedicated team of corporate photographers are amongst the very best in the region, and have earned a stellar reputation as the leaders and pioneers in product photography, mouth-watering food photography, contemporary interior photography, and professional headshot photography in Dubai and Abu Dhabi.

EDGE with partnership with Aver introduced Strategic Tracking with the release of the PTC500S Professional Auto Tracking Camera earlier this year. Strategic Tracking incorporates three uniquely designed target tracking configurations that allow for precision tracking for perfectly captured video in a variety of different shooting environments, without the need of a professional operator. Our passion for photography started as an avid backpackers off the beaten track though we continued exploring new places and corners of the city therefore we are passionate about capturing authentic moments and feelings.

Based at our Dubai studio, we work closely with a talented hair and make-up artists, luxury event planners, creative directors, and professional editors to tell your brand’s story through beautiful and engaging imagery. We deliver unparalleled quality, creativity, and reliability with an exceptionally high level of service that helps us nurture life-long relationships with our clients. At EDGE, we absolutely love what we do and certainly can’t imagine doing anything
